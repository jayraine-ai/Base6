Task 1 (21 marks)

Information

Canteen feedback needed

I am concerned that not enough people are using the canteen so I want to make some changes.

Please write me a report telling me what you think about the food as well as the service and environment.

I also want your ideas for any improvements we could make.

Your report should include sections on:

  • the food
  • the service and the appearance (tables, decoration, etc.)
  • suggested improvements.
Lottie Smith
The Canteen Manager.

Writing Task

Write a report for Lottie Smith about the canteen. In your report, you should:

  • say what you like and dislike about the food
  • comment on the service and appearance
  • suggest improvements which could be made.

Aim to write about 200 to 250 words.
