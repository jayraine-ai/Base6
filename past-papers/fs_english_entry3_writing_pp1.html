Question 4 (14 marks)

Write an article for a local magazine about a music event.

You should include:

  • where the event was held
  • how long the event lasted
  • what you liked about the event and why
  • what you didn't like about the event and why.

Write at least 8 sentences. These must include at least 3 compound sentences that use conjunctions, for example: and, but, also, because. Use at least 2 paragraphs.
