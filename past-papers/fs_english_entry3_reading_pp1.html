Improving your English
by Abbas

Being able to communicate clearly is so important. I always wanted to improve my skill in writing letters, stories and emails and have had some success.

How could you improve?

There are various things you can do to become excellent at English. You could:

  • join an English class
  • use a dictionary
  • re-read your work to check for errors
  • experiment with different words.

The more writing you do, the better you will become. Make time to practise your writing.

My English teacher, Ms Wicks, has helped me a lot. She taught me how to spell and helped me to prepare for tests.

These days, I feel more confident about my English skills.

Good luck!
Abbas
