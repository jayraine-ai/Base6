Question 11

Ben is the manager of a leisure centre. He has this formula to work out the volume of the water in the pool.

V = 10dw + 15aw

where

  • V = the volume of the water in the pool (m³)
  • d = the depth of the water at the deep end of the pool (m)
  • w = width of the pool (m)
  • a = the depth of the water at the shallow end of the pool (m)

Ben knows the width of the pool is 12 m. The depth of the water at the deep end is 2 m. The depth of the water at the shallow end is 1.5 m.

Ben uses two water pumps at the same time to empty the pool. Pump A empties water at a rate of 31.2 m³ per hour. Pump B empties water at a rate of 27 m³ per hour.

Ben thinks that using the two water pumps will empty the pool in less than 9 hours.

Is he correct? Show why you think this. (5 marks)

Total for Question 11 is 5 marks
