Word of the Week
cultivate
/ ˈkʌl · tɪ · veɪt /  •  verb
verb — meaning 1
To prepare land and grow plants or crops on it.
"Farmers have cultivated this valley for hundreds of years."
verb — meaning 2
To develop or improve something carefully over time — like a skill, a friendship, or a good habit.
"She worked hard to cultivate her talent for music."
verb — meaning 3
To build a friendly relationship with someone — often because it is useful to you.
"He made efforts to cultivate friendships with his new classmates."
