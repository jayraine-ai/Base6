Ways to research 🗺️

There are three ways you can gather information for this project. Here's a quick guide to each one.

🌐
Internet search
Try searching for your topic + "explained simply" or "for young people".

Look for .org, .gov or .edu sites — they're often more reliable.

If something seems surprising, check whether another site says the same thing.
🗣️
Interviewing someone
Think about who might know something useful — a family member, local person, or someone with relevant experience.

Write 2 or 3 questions before you speak to them.

You don't need to record it — notes or memory is fine.
🎬
Documentary or video
YouTube, BBC iPlayer and Netflix all have good options.

You don't have to watch the whole thing — even 10 minutes can give you useful ideas.

Pause when something interesting comes up and jot it down.
💡 You don't need to use all three. One or two good sources is plenty — quality matters more than quantity.
