⚡ Contact and Non-Contact Forces

What is a force?

A force is a push or a pull. Forces can make things start moving, stop moving, speed up, slow down, or change direction. Every force has a size (how strong it is) and a direction (which way it pushes or pulls).

Two types of force

👋 Contact forces

These only work when two things are touching. The force happens at the point of contact.

  • Friction — slows things down when surfaces rub
  • Air resistance — pushes back against moving objects
  • Normal force — a surface pushing back up against an object
  • Tension — a pulling force through a rope or string

🌀 Non-contact forces

These work without touching — they can act across a gap or even empty space.

  • Gravity — pulls objects towards each other
  • Magnetism — attracts or repels magnetic materials
  • Static electricity — attracts or repels charged objects
