What is a Trajectory?

The Flight Path of Things

A trajectory is simply the imaginary line or path that an object traces as it flies, leaps, or shoots through the air.

Whenever something is thrown or launched into the sky, it can't go straight forever. Gravity pulls it down, shaping its path into a beautiful smooth curve.
