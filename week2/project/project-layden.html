💡 Pricing Tips

🎯 Keep it simple

Round numbers like 50p, £1, £1.50 are easier to pay and easier to give change for.

⚖️ Be fair

Charge enough to raise good money, but not so much that people won't buy. £1 per item is usually a safe bet.

📦 Bundle deals

Offer a deal — like 3 for £2 — and people often buy more than they planned!

🏷️ Labelling Tips

📝 Name it clearly

Write the name of each bake on a label — "Cupcake", "Rocky Road", etc.

⚠️ List allergens

Add a short list: "Contains: gluten, dairy, eggs". Use green labels for vegan/free-from items.

💚 Highlight free-from

Put a special sticker on the vegan and gluten-free items — people with allergies will really appreciate it.
