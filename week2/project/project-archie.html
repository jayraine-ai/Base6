😊 Check In

💡 Exploring Ideas

It's completely okay not to have your idea locked in yet — that's what this week is for. Let's explore what interests you.

🎬 Short film or movie

Write and film a short story — could be funny, dramatic, action-packed, anything.

📸 Photography project

Take a series of photos around a theme — could be nature, people, places, abstract.

🎙️ Documentary

Film a short documentary about something or someone interesting — a place, a hobby, a story.

🎭 Stop motion animation

Create an animated short using objects, figures or drawings — frame by frame.

📖 Photo story or zine

Tell a story using a combination of photos and words — like a photo comic or mini magazine.

✨ Something else

Got your own idea? Go for it — describe it below.

Even vague thoughts or half-ideas are worth writing down — sometimes the best projects start from a feeling rather than a clear plan.

🌱 Early Ideas

Start with "My project will be about..." or "I want to make something that..." — it doesn't have to be perfect.

💭 Reflection

Even if your idea isn't fully formed yet, think about what message or feeling your project might leave with an audience.
