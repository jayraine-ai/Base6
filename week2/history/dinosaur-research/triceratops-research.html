✅ Tip: The correct spelling is Triceratops — a capital T and no extra letters. A very common mistake is writing "Triceritops" — check your spelling before exporting.
Step 1 · Warm-up fact check

Are these Triceratops claims true?

Read each claim. Use what you know — or look it up — then pick your answer.

Triceratops had three horns: two long brow horns and one shorter nose horn.

Look at the model you built — count the horns and notice their different lengths.

Triceratops was a meat-eater that hunted other dinosaurs.

Think about what those horns and that beak-shaped mouth would have been used for.

Scientists think the frill on Triceratops was used only as armour to protect its neck.

Research what else the frill might have been used for — there are several theories.

Triceratops lived at the same time as Tyrannosaurus rex.

Check the time periods for both dinosaurs — they both appear at the end of the Cretaceous.

Triceratops was one of the last non-bird dinosaurs to exist before the mass extinction.

Check what happened 66 million years ago and which dinosaurs were alive just before that event.

Triceratops walked on two legs like T. rex.

Look at your model — how many legs does it stand on? How does its body shape compare to T. rex?

⚠️ Common mistake: Many people write Triceritops. The correct spelling is Triceratops — "cerat" not "cerit". Double-check before you finish your poster.

📝 Fill in your notes

💡 Starter: The name comes from three Greek words. Look up "tri", "cerat" and "ops" — what does each part mean?
💡 Starter: It lived near the end of the age of dinosaurs — search for dates around 68–66 million years ago.
💡 Starter: Most fossils come from western North America — look for states like Montana, Wyoming, and South Dakota.
💡 Starter: Check its length in metres. It was one of the largest horned dinosaurs — compare it to a modern rhinoceros.
💡 Think about its three horns and the large bony frill around its neck. What might these have been used for?
Do not copy and paste from websites. Read, close the tab, then write what you remember.
💡 Ideas: the debate about frill colour, whether Triceratops lived in herds, or how its horns grew as it aged.
Paste the web address or write the full website name. At least two required.
⚠️ Watch out: Some sources say Triceratops used its horns mainly for fighting T. rex. Scientists now think they were also used for display and recognising other members of their species — check for this debate.
