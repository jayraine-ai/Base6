🌱 Why Personal Hygiene Matters

Everyone goes through change

As we grow up, our bodies change — and so do our hygiene needs. This isn't something to be embarrassed about. It happens to every single person on the planet. The good news is that taking care of yourself is straightforward once you know what to do and why.

Personal hygiene isn't about being perfect. It's about feeling comfortable in your own skin — and showing respect for yourself and the people around you.

🧠 It's connected to how you feel

Research shows that personal hygiene and self-care have a direct impact on how confident and comfortable we feel. When we feel clean and well-groomed, we tend to feel more confident, more focused, and more ready to take on the day.

It also matters socially — being aware of how we present ourselves is part of understanding and respecting others around us.

2. "Deodorant and antiperspirant are the same thing."

3. "You should brush your teeth immediately after eating."

4. "Sweating more means you're less clean."
