Big Idea2 / 13

What is area?

Area is the amount of space inside a flat shape — like how much carpet you'd need to cover a floor.

We measure area in square units — like cm² or m².

The Formula3 / 13

Finding the area of a rectangle

You don't have to count every square — there's a shortcut!

Area = length × width
Multiply the two sides together.
