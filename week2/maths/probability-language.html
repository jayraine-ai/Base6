What is Probability?

🎲 Chance and likelihood

Probability is the chance that something will happen. Some things are very likely to happen. Some things are very unlikely. And some things are somewhere in between.

We use special words to describe how likely something is. These words sit on a scale — from impossible all the way to certain.

📏 The Probability Scale

Imagine a line. On the left is impossible — it will never happen. On the right is certain — it will definitely happen. Everything else sits somewhere in between.

0 0.25 0.5 0.75 1
Impossible Unlikely Even chance Likely Certain

📖 What do the words mean?

Impossible — will never happen. e.g. rolling a 7 on a standard dice
Unlikely — could happen but probably won't. e.g. it snowing in July
Even chance — equally likely to happen or not. e.g. flipping heads on a coin
Likely — probably will happen. e.g. it being light outside at noon
Certain — will definitely happen. e.g. the sun rising tomorrow
