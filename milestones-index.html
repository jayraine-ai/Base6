About this tracker

How it works

Each tracker is password protected — only you can open yours. Inside, you'll find 15 milestones covering every subject area this term. Tap any milestone to mark it as done. Your progress saves automatically in your browser. When you're ready, you can share your progress with Jay directly from the tracker.

Your password

Your password is just your first name in lowercase — for example, layden, leon, leo, archie, or harrison. If you get stuck, ask Jay.
