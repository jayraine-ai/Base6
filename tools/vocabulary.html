How this version supports different learners

Autism-friendly: predictable card layout, literal labels, low visual noise, no timers, and no surprise animations.

Dyslexia-friendly: larger text, wider spacing, off-white background, strong contrast, and short chunked meanings.

PDA-friendly: choice-based wording, optional practice, reset/show-all controls, and no forced sequence.

ADHD-friendly: search-first design, clear filters, one-word focus mode, read-aloud, and quick random practice.
