Trips and visits 📍

Sometimes the best research happens when you go somewhere. Here are some places near Yeovil that might connect with the project.
Tap any that feel worth exploring. You can add your own ideas too.

✈️
Fleet Air Arm Museum, Yeovilton
How has military technology changed over time? How do those choices still affect us?
🚗
Haynes Motor Museum, Sparkford
The story of transport choices — from early cars to electric vehicles and what comes next.
🏰
Montacute House (National Trust)
400 years of choices about land, wealth and power — and how they shaped the local area.
🍎
Local Farm or Food Producer
Where does food come from? How do farming choices affect the land and the future?
🌳
Ninesprings / Yeovil Country Park
A green space that exists because of choices made by the town. What keeps places like this going?
🛍️
Yeovil Town Centre
Charity shops, independent businesses — how do where we shop and what we buy shape the high street?
♻️
Recycling Centre / Repair Café
Waste, repair, reuse — how do individual choices add up across a whole community?
🎨
Sherborne or Bruton — galleries
How does art respond to the world and capture ideas about the future? Bruton has some brilliant small galleries.
🌾
Somerset Rural Life Museum, Glastonbury
How has life in Somerset changed over 200 years? What choices drove those changes?
📚
Yeovil Library or Local Archive
Old newspapers and photographs can show how people made decisions — and what happened next.
