💡 AI Inventor's Workshop

Every AI in the world started as somebody's idea. Today the workshop is yours: invent an AI helper for something you actually care about. There are no wrong answers — just decisions only an inventor can make.

The inventor's rule: every real AI needs two things — examples to learn from (its training data) and something to predict or decide. Your design will need both. Everything else is up to you.
