🕵️ AI Detective

Artificial intelligence is hiding in plain sight, all through an ordinary day. Your case: follow the timeline and decide where AI is at work — and where it's just regular technology doing what it's told.

Your detective briefing

First, know your suspect. AI — artificial intelligence — is computer software that learns patterns from lots of examples, then uses those patterns to make predictions or decisions about new things it hasn't seen before.

Ordinary technology follows fixed instructions a human wrote, the same way every time. A toaster toasts. A calculator calculates. They never learn anything.

The detective's question: "Is this machine learning from examples and making predictions — or just following the same instructions every time?" If it learns and predicts, you've found AI.
