🏥 Health Centre — The Concept

✅ Decisions made: the app is called Health Centre, and it supports young people with both mental AND physical wellbeing. That's the foundation sorted — now we build on it.

Mental could be things like mood check-ins or calming exercises. Physical could be step challenges, sleep, stretches...

🪙 The Reward Currency — Design Lab

💡 Your big idea: users earn in-app currency for engaging with wellbeing activities, and can exchange it for real gift cards. Real apps do this — Sweatcoin pays people in points for steps. So this is a genuinely commercial idea. Let's design it properly.
Activity in the appCoins earned
😊 Daily mood check-in
🫁 Completing a calming exercise
🚶 Hitting a daily step goal
📓 Writing a journal entry
🔥 A 7-day streak bonus
A user doing everything daily for a week would earn roughly 365 coins.

🧠 The Hard Questions (every app designer faces these)

A rewards system is a powerful tool — and powerful tools need careful design. Have a think about these. There are no wrong answers, only trade-offs.

💷 Who pays for the gift cards?

Real money has to come from somewhere. Some apps charge a subscription, some show adverts, some get sponsors (a sports brand might sponsor step challenges). Sweatcoin uses advertisers and partners.

🎭 The motivation puzzle

If people only do breathing exercises to farm coins, are they actually getting calmer? Designers call this "gaming the system". Some apps cap daily coins, or make streaks (consistency) worth more than bulk activity.

⚖️ The fairness question

A step-goal reward is easy for some people and impossible for others — different bodies, different situations. Great wellbeing apps let users set personal goals rather than one bar for everyone.

🎨 Design and Progress

Think about what would make it feel calm and safe to use. Where does the coin balance show? What does the rewards shop screen look like?
