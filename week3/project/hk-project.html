🔗 Your Joining Methods

You chose three: glue, wire, and metal welding. Smart order to try them:

1️⃣ Glue first

Easiest and safest. Metal needs the right glue (epoxy works on metal; ask Jay) and patience while it dries.

2️⃣ Wire backup

Your own predicted fix! Wire wrapping is strong, adjustable, and you can redo it. Cover the twisted ends with tape.

3️⃣ Welding = adult job

The strongest joint, but it's hot metal work — adult-run only, full PPE, and only if it's truly needed.
